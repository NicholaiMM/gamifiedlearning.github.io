What We Do

Our Ministry Areas

Serving the body of Christ and our community through intentional, Spirit-led ministry in every area of life.

🎵

Worship & Praise

Lifting our voices to God in spirit and in truth. Our worship team creates an atmosphere of encounter every Sunday morning and Wednesday night.

🌍

Community Outreach

Serving our neighbors through food drives, homeless outreach, and local partnerships — the hands and feet of Jesus in our city.

🙏

Prayer & Intercession

A dedicated prayer team standing in the gap for individuals, families, our city, and the nations — 24 hours a week.

⭐

Youth & Children

Building the next generation of faith-filled leaders. Age-appropriate programs from nursery through college that make God's Word come alive.

📡

Media & Broadcasting

Reaching the world through online sermons, podcasts, and live streaming — the voice of Jesus through every platform and channel.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Ministry

Strengthening marriages and families through counseling, classes, and small groups built on biblical foundations of love and commitment.

You Are Welcome Here

Weekly Services & Gatherings

Come as you are. Every gathering is an opportunity to experience God's presence, grow in your faith, and find your community.

Sunday 9 AM

Morning Worship Service

Our main weekend gathering — praise, prayer, and a message from God's Word. Children's ministry available for all ages.

Sunday 11 AM

Second Morning Service

A second opportunity to worship and grow together. Same message, same heart — for those who need an alternative time.

Wed 7 PM

Midweek Bible Study & Prayer

An intimate midweek gathering for deeper study of Scripture and corporate intercession. Youth ministry runs simultaneously.

Fri 6:30 PM

Youth Night

A high-energy gathering for teens and young adults — worship, games, small groups, and a relevant message for the next generation.

Sat 8 AM

Early Morning Prayer

Start your Saturday in God's presence. One hour of corporate prayer and worship to fuel your week and intercede for the nations.

What We Believe

Our Core Pillars of Faith

Rooted in the eternal truths of Scripture, these pillars shape everything we do and who we are.

📖

Scripture

The Bible is God's inspired, living Word — our ultimate authority for faith, life, and practice.

❤️

Love

We love God with all our heart and love our neighbor as ourselves — this is the greatest commandment.

🕊️

Grace

Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — a free gift no one earns or deserves.

🔥

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, empowering believers for life and service.

🌐

Missions

The Great Commission compels us to make disciples of all nations — locally, regionally, and globally.

🤝

Community

We were made for one another — authentic, life-giving community is essential to following Jesus.
